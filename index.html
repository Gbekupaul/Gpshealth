About World Safety Organisation

Our goal is to create a healthy work free from hazard enviroment, where staffs can enjoy hours of carrying out their task effectively and with ease.

WHAT YOU GAIN OUT OF THE PROGRAM

This program is designed to give you knowlegde that will help ensure the protection of employees, equipment and the enviroment.

You will also learn how to reduce absenteeism arising from the workplace.

You will learn how to increase productivity at the workplace by following health and safety guidelines and maintaining a safe work place that will reduce the risk of work-related illness and injuries.
